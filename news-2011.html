May 12

Exothermic Robotics Teams Win Trophies at VEX World Championship

Team 575 (Haiku) won first place for their Design a Game Computer Animation Online Challenge, in which they designed a game called "Ring Rolled." Team 10D (Imension) won the Technology Division Build Award. This year, all the Exothermic teams qualified for Worlds, although one team chose not to go. With seven teams attending Worlds, EXO was one of the largest single-club contingents at the competition.

May 11

2011 Online Challenge Winners Announcement

The Online Challenge results have come out! The judges had a really hard time choosing the top 3 for each category. Exothermic Robotics placed in two different challenges, with Team 575 (Exothermic Haiku) winning first place for the Design A Game Challenge with their "Ring Rolled" game, and the overall club placing second for their "How to Build a Drive-Train" RECF educational video.
